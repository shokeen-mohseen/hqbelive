What safety measures should I take while travelling?

  • Keep valuables like cash, travellers cheque, passport and ticket in your handbag or luggage at all times.
  • Do not keep all the money, travellers cheques and credit cards in one wallet/purse or pocket. Disperse them at different places so that you won't be penniless in the case of theft or misplacement of wallet.
  • Hold your bags cautiously or keep them fastened at crowded places like stations or sightseeing spots.
  • Avoid carrying too much money during sightseeing or lone excursions.  Never leave your bags unattended, even in the coach.
  • Pack your baggage yourself and label it with your name and contact details for easy identification.
  • Do not accept any package from strangers or co-passengers.
  • Ensure that your check-in bags have a locking facility.

Booking and cancellation

What happens if a activity booking is Rejected/Failed?

When the activity booking gets rejected/failed, the refund is initiated immediately. Please allow your bank 02 to 07 days to credit the amount to your account.

What happens if I cancel or do not show up for an activity booked?

Make sure that you read the cancellation policy carefully for the activity booked. Cancellation charges may apply, the remaining money will be refunded to your mode of payment once you cancel the booking with us.   No money will be refunded in case of a no-show.

When do I receive confirmation of my activity booking?

The confirmation process depends on the booking conditions. These are explained in the Additional Information field located on the activity information page. Most activities are confirmed at time of booking. However, others will be advised to you within 12-48 hours. If confirmation or otherwise is not received within 48 hours, please contact on care@hqbe.in for further assistance.

Can I change the booking dates?

Date can be changed 30 days prior to the travel date, for the stay arrangement only; however any booking related to activities/ excursions/ travel tickets cannot be modified. The stay changes are possible as per the availability with the supplier and the terms and conditions applicable.

Can I cancel my holiday tour midway?

Yes, you can. But, the amount paid for the package would be non-refundable.
